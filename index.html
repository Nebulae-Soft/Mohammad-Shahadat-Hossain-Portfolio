Expertise

    I focus on setting a clear product vision that aligns with both user needs and business goals. I enjoy connecting the dots between customer problems, market trends, and company strategy to shape products that matter.

  • Roadmapping & Prioritization

    I translate big-picture ideas into actionable roadmaps. I use frameworks like RICE and OKRs to prioritize features that create the most value, while staying flexible enough to adapt to new insights and changes.

  • User Research & Customer Insights

    Talking to users and understanding their needs is at the core of how I work. I gather insights through interviews, surveys, and testing sessions to ensure we’re solving the right problems in the right way.

    I rely on data to validate decisions and spot opportunities. Whether it's digging into product metrics, running A/B tests, or analyzing user behavior, I use data to guide the product forward with confidence.
